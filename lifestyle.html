LONDON MARATHON

When: April
Location: Red start
The biggest event of long distance running is organized every spring, in April most commontly.Founded in 1981 by the former Olympic champion Chris Basher and athlete John Disley,the marathon today attracts more than thirty million professional amateur runners.

::after

THE BOAT RACE

When: First week of april Location: River Thames The popular rowing race between the students of United Kingdom's famous universities takes place every first week of April and is commonly known as the boat race. it starts from Putney Bridge, passing various traditional landmarks and ending near Barnes Railway Bridges and Chiswick Bridge.
